4 Lead Creation — Fields Collected

Owner, Admin, and Sales Manager click "+ New Lead" to open the create form. A unique Lead ID is auto-generated on save.

Client Name Required

🆔 Unique Lead ID (e.g. LD-7823)
• Lead enters New pipeline column
• Tagged with assigned_account_role
• Stored in browser localStorage
• Timestamp auto-recorded
🗂️ History & Trash
All created leads can be:
• Moved to Trash (recoverable)
• Recovered from Trash Bin
• Deleted Forever (permanent)
